Jonny McIntosh

I'm a Stipendiary Lecturer in Philosophy at Balliol, Oriel, and St. Edmund Hall, Oxford. I work on the philosophy of logic and language and related issues in the history of philosophy, epistemology, ethics, metaphysics, and the philosophy of mind and cognitive science. This website mainly contains reading lists, lecture slides, and handouts I use for teaching.
